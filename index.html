About

Our team was started in 2019 by a group of coding and ethical hacking enthusiasts with the aim of training fellow students with such common interests. This is a great platform to learn, share and explore!



What we actually do......
Ethical Hacking and Cybersecurity

Our main objective is to participate in bug bounty competitions and securing networks.To ensure safety and security, we should first know the loopholes in our system. For this, we train and well equip our team members with various techniques of ethical hacking.
Web Development

To break into a website, it is important to know how it was built. We therefore ensure all our team members are aware of the basics of web designing and development.
Languages

Proficiency in computer languages is cery important as they form the very basics of web designing and hacking. Proper guidance and doubt sessions are provided to train all the members in HTML, CSS, C, C++ and many more.






Projects
Here is a sneak peek at some of the many projects our members are working on.....
WiFi Pumpkin

This project aims to create awareness that it is not safe to connect to open networks and surf http sites.
Low-Space Bad USB Script

It is a tool which is detected by the computer as a keyboard and executes commands with super fast speed and uses just 4kB of space.
Workshops

Many workshops are conducted to build interest among the students. We also actively participate in organising events during Technozion and other fests.
